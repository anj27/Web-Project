What is 'Fantasy Football?'
Fantasy Football is a game that people can play on their phones.
To play the game, a person would just have to download a phone.
In the game, they can choose real-life players to earn 'fantasy points' for them.
For every week, you will be matched against another fantasy player.
The team that scores more fantasy points will win.
We have created a database of all the top fantasy players, split by position.
